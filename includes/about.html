WHAT?
RompЯ is all about discovery. Discovering more about the music you love and discovering music you don't know.

WHY?
The collectioniser aims to do what Amarok 1.4 used to do with my music collection. The looks aim to do what Amarok 2 does with my music collection. It's an MPD and Mopidy client because my media center is over there. Why is it called RompЯ? It just is. I think it was going to be an acronym but I gave up at 'R' and then I liked the idea of using a 'Я' at the end.

WHERE?
RompЯ is developed on Mac OS X. My media centre runs Mythbuntu and Kodi and is over there. I generally run Mopidy instead of MPD, because it's great.
